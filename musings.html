July 31st, 2019

Om Krishna

Every form of happiness is private. Our greatest moments are personal, self motivated, not to
be touched. The things which are sacred or precious to us are things we withdraw from sharing.
I wouldn't call this selfishness or egoism - these words have been perverted. I probably think I
dont know the word for the kind of self sufficiency I mean.
A self sufficient ego is probably the only quality I demand, and appreciate. Nothing else.

July 28th, 2019

Om Krishna

He gave his friends a silent look
Entertaining every thought around
Yet their story seemed like from a book
Including within romance drama dance and sound.
An epic could be made,he felt
Penning love was ever beautiful
One letter at a time,he spelt
Lad seemed adorable but the gal was dutiful.
Only often he thought it was too soon
Gee other stuff were happening too
In no way would their story be immune
Someone nevertheless would be to their rescue.
Experience an aw for this atleast, here's a poem for you.
