December 16th, 2019

Om Krishna

So I guess this is the new normal, that one or another part of this country will continuously burn, and that these fires will be wilfully started.
An incompetent government is one that cannot take a country or soceity towards stability and progress. It takes a malicious intent to push a relatively stable country into the throes of violence.
If the extreme brutality of what is happening at Jamia and AMU is anything to go by, what we are seeing is a vicious display of naked power, which is bent on putting people, a particular class of people, in their place in this country. All while more hate is being dog-whistled. This is planned, had been planned for many years, perhaps.
What coincidences of birth and time have ensured that Jamia is not happening to me, or my university? And how long does one bank on these coincidences to save oneself?

July 31st, 2019

Om Krishna

Every form of happiness is private. Our greatest moments are personal, self motivated, not to be touched. The things which are sacred or precious to us are things we withdraw from sharing. I wouldn't call this selfishness or egoism - these words have been perverted. I probably think I dont know the word for the kind of self sufficiency I mean. A self sufficient ego is probably the only quality I demand, and appreciate. Nothing else.
