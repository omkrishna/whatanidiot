It was one of my this year's New Year's Resolution thing (not really, it has been there for the last 4 years) - to read and write more. And that is where this blog comes into picture. The purpose of this blog is to give you that reading time everyday - either suggest you a great book, or an amazing article, or some important piece of news. Occasionally you might have to bear with some of my own musings, but you can always ignore them :)
There are plenty of amazing apps and websites and people who publish wonderful content everyday, content that I believe can impact our day to day lives and make a difference. But. But we seem to suffer from the problem of plenty when it comes to read. The problem with so many of us isnt that we dont have stuff to read, its a bit more complicated than that - we have too much stuff to read, so much so that even thinking about all of them makes us nauseous and we postpone reading altogether.
